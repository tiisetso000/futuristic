ABOUT US

Futuristicroots is a media company that was established in 2017 by Phillemon Moetjie. The concept for the Futuristicroots is influenced by the increased demand for media services by small businesses and brands with the potential to succeed in the market but lacking the necessary media skills and resources to compete with larger brands.
